DONATION INFORMATION

Where would you be without your education? Please consider sponsoring a student and changing the trajectory of their life!

You can click the “Donate” button below to donate online, call or email us to make a pledge, or send us a donation via mail.

The STAR Sponsorship Program is a non-profit 501(c)(3) corporation, as defined by the Internal Revenue Code. Contributions to the STAR Sponsorship Program, Inc. are fully tax-deductible.

If you are interested in pledging a gift, please contact us via phone or email. If you would like to send a donation, you can find our address below.

Giving options 

  • $2,500 provides a student with a scholarship, educational counseling and program support
  • $2,200 provides a scholarship for one student for an entire academic year with academic support

(100% of gifts allocated towards scholarship will be applied towards scholarships, unless allocated otherwise.)

Additional Giving Options 

  • Allocate any amount of your gift towards operations/program support
  • Allocate any amount of your gift towards student needs (computers, uniforms, camps, etc.)
  • Unrestricted–use where needed

Corporations and Foundations

Corporations can sponsor a group of students and allocate their employees to mentor these students. Companies will receive report cards and correspondence from their students.

Matching Gifts

Many companies provide a matching gift program for their employees that increases the value of a gift. Perhaps your company is one of those. Contact your human resources department for more information.

Memorial Gifts

Making a contribution in memory of someone is a beautiful way to honor that person and celebrate his or her life. STAR is honored to be included in such tributes as are the children and families we serve. Acknowledgement of your choosing will be provided to share with the loved ones of the person you are remembering.

Planned Giving

Contribute to our future through planned giving. There are many ways to provide for future donations to STAR Sponsorship Pgm., Inc. including your will. Please have your attorney contact us by email or call 817-888-3255.

Marketable Securities

Gifts of securities either held by your bank or brokerage or held by you offer significant tax advantages and are easy to make. Please contact us for information about gifts of securities or mutual funds.
