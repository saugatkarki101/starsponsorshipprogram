GIVING PARENTS A CHOICE AND CHILDREN A CHANCE

This is a private scholarship program for at-risk/lower income students whose parents want more for their child in education. Their local public school is not providing what their child needs to succeed. They commit to STAR program expectations of being on time, completing homework, respecting teachers and fellow students and working towards an honor roll report card.

NEWSLETTER

Quarterly, STAR publishes a newsletter that keeps donors up to date on our progress as a program. The newsletters also give some highlights of the past quarter of news on the program, supporters, and events.
